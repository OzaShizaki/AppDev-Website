Course Overview

This course covers application development and emerging technologies that are driving innovation in the field of computing. Through the course, students will develop practical skills, analyze technological impact, and adapt to industry trends.

Course Objectives:

  • Understand Core Principles
  • Explore Emerging Technologies
  • Develop Practical Skills
  • Analyze Impact and Trends

Learning Outcomes:

  • Design and Develop Applications
  • Implement Emerging Technologies
  • Evaluate Technological Impact
  • Adapt to Industry Trends
